Arjun: Hey Aarav, I heard you decided to attend that protest next week. Aren't you worried about getting in trouble?

Aarav: Not really, Arjun. We live in a democracy where we have the right to express our opinions. The right to freedom of speech and expression allows us to participate in peaceful protests.

Arjun: That's true. But what if someone tries to stop you or says you shouldn't be there?

Aarav: They can't legally stop me unless I'm breaking the law. As long as I'm peaceful, the law protects my right to be there. That's the beauty of our freedom.

Arjun: You're right, Aarav. It's important to stand up for what we believe in, especially when we have the freedom to do so.
